What does it take to become a trainee?

To apply for a trainee position with ICT Solutions' web development team, candidates should have a good understanding of basic web development concepts. This means knowing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and having an interest in design and making websites user-friendly. Knowing tools like React or Angular might also be useful. Trainees should be good at solving problems, paying attention to details, and be ready to learn in a fast-paced industry. Being able to communicate well and work in a team is important for working with senior developers and other team members. Having a passion for being creative, innovative, and always improving is also important for success and growth in web development.
