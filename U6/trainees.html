What does it take to become a trainee?

To become a trainee with ICT Solutions' web development team, aspiring candidates need a strong foundation in web development fundamentals. This includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with a keen eye for design and user experience. Familiarity with popular web development frameworks like React or Angular can be beneficial. Trainees should also possess problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a willingness to learn and adapt in a fast-paced industry. Excellent communication and teamwork abilities are essential for collaborating with senior developers and other team members. A passion for innovation, creativity, and a drive to continuously improve one's skills are key traits that can help individuals succeed and grow within the web development field.
